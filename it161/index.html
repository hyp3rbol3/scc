About Me

Hi! I'm Alexander Herskovic, a visual artist and Web Development student at Seattle Central College. Born in Houston, Texas to Chilean immigrants, I moved to Seattle in 2022 and have been enjoying the PNW weather since.

As a visual artist, I focus on themes of gender, sexuality, and expression, and incorporates elements of my Chilean and Jewish identity into works about queerness and transgender experience. I focus primarily on printmaking, and you can find my art here.

As a web developer, my main focus is on creating clean, bloat-free user experiences through quality coding. I'm looking forward to learning more about JavaScript and frontend frameworks like React.

When I'm not making art or coding, you can find me practicing Historical European Martial Arts at Lonin League, or playing tabletop games with friends.
